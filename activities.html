Talk to other women

Creating a welcoming enviornment where all women can freely talk and share their views on the topic of sexual harassment is the primary purpose of this product.Start chatting with other women and feel good about each other. This app allows you to chat through voice and video calls and text messages too. An online platform where you can connect to several women from all around India and talk to them whenever you want to without needing to step out.


Take self-defense classes

Connect with people you feel comfortable with and be confident about what you do. Don't be discouraged to do things that interest you and fulfill your dreams.
